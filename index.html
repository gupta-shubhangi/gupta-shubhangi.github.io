Shubhangi Gupta

Ph.D. candidate in Digital Media at Georgia Tech

I'm actively looking for full-time positions post graduation. ✨

My research aims to develop methods that support the public in critically engaging with predictive technologies that affect their lives in dominant yet invisible ways. I draw on participatory methods integrated with STS (Science and Technology Studies) scholarship to understand how different publics envision the workings and impacts of predictive systems in relation to their communities and neighborhoods.

I am advised by Dr. Yanni Loukissas and am funded by the Women, Science, and Technology Learning Community at Georgia Tech.
