I am a Ph.D. candidate in Digital Media at Georgia Tech. I am advised by Dr. Yanni Loukissas as part of the Local Data Design Lab. I am funded by the Women, Science, and Technology Learning Community at Georgia Tech.

I graduated from the MS-HCI program at Georgia Tech in May 2020 got my undergraduate degree in May 2018 from the Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati where I majored in Interaction Design.

Research and Background

My research draws upon feminist and STS scholarship integrated with qualitative and participatory methods to support diverse publics in critically engaging with predictive technologies and assess these tools in relation to themselves and their communities

In support of my research interests, I have interned at IBM's Responsible and Inclusive Tech team (summer 2022) and Google's Ads Privacy and Safety team (fall 2021). I also identify as an Interaction designer and user researcher. In the past, I have interned as a UX designer and researcher at Striim inc. (summer 2019), LRI at Université Paris-Sud (summer 2017), Microsoft Research India (spring 2017) and IDeaS Lab IISc Bangalore (summer 2016). I have had the honor of working with some great scholars in the field of Human-Computer Interaction and Design such as Dr. Michel Beaudouin-Lafon, Dr. Wendy Mackay, Dr. Amaresh Chakrabarti and Dr. Pankaj Upadhyay.
